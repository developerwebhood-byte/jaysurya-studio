FAQ

Contact & Consultation FAQs

Common questions about getting started with Jaysurya Studio.

Is the consultation really free? +
Yes, completely free. Our initial growth consultation is a no-obligation session where we audit your current digital presence, discuss your goals, and outline a strategy. There's no pressure or hard sell — just actionable insights you can use immediately.
How quickly will you respond to my inquiry? +
We respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours. For urgent matters, you can reach us directly by phone or WhatsApp and we'll respond even faster.
What happens after I submit the contact form? +
After submitting, you'll receive an email confirmation. One of our senior growth consultants will reach out within 24 hours to schedule a discovery call. On that call, we'll learn about your business, share an initial audit, and discuss how Growth Engine could work for you.
Do you work with businesses outside India? +
Yes, we work with clients across India, the UAE, UK, and other markets. Our team is fully equipped for remote collaboration. All strategy sessions, reporting, and communication happen online, making it easy to work with us from anywhere.
What information should I have ready for the consultation? +
Come prepared with your business goals, current marketing budget (rough range is fine), your website URL, and any specific challenges you're facing. It also helps to know your target customer profile. But don't worry — we'll guide you through everything during the call.
Can I visit your office in person? +
Absolutely. We welcome in-person meetings at our office Monday to Friday between 9:00 AM and 6:00 PM IST. Please schedule an appointment in advance by calling us or emailing hello@jaysuryastudio.com so we can ensure the right team members are available.
