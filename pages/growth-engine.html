FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Growth Engine before getting started.

What exactly is the Growth Engine service? +
Growth Engine is Jaysurya Studio's flagship, fully managed digital growth service. It combines SEO, website optimization, lead generation, marketing automation, analytics, content strategy, and monthly strategy consulting into a single, integrated system designed to deliver predictable, compounding business growth.
How long does it take to see results? +
Most clients start seeing measurable improvements in website traffic and lead generation within the first 60–90 days. SEO results typically compound over 3–6 months. Marketing automation and conversion improvements often show impact within the first 30 days. We set realistic timelines during your onboarding consultation.
Is Growth Engine suitable for small businesses? +
Absolutely. Growth Engine is designed to scale with your business. We serve startups, SMEs, and enterprise clients alike. Our team customizes the scope, priorities, and budget allocation based on your current stage and goals so you're never paying for things you don't need yet.
What industries do you specialize in? +
We have deep expertise in Healthcare, Education, Real Estate, E-commerce, SaaS, Professional Services, Manufacturing, and Local Businesses. Our frameworks are adapted to each industry's buyer journey, compliance requirements, and competitive landscape.
Do you offer a contract or month-to-month engagement? +
We offer both options. Month-to-month gives you flexibility, while a 6 or 12-month commitment unlocks better pricing and allows us to execute deeper, longer-term strategies. We recommend at least a 6-month commitment to see the full power of Growth Engine compound over time.
How do you measure and report success? +
We set up a custom analytics dashboard tracking your key metrics: organic traffic, keyword rankings, lead volume, conversion rates, cost per lead, and ROI. You receive a detailed monthly report and attend two strategy sessions per month where we review performance and plan the next actions.
Will you need access to our existing website and tools? +
Yes, we'll need access to your website CMS, Google Analytics, Google Search Console, and any existing CRM or marketing tools. Our onboarding process includes a secure access handover and integration audit. We work within your existing tech stack or recommend improvements where needed.
How is Growth Engine different from hiring a freelancer or agency? +
Unlike freelancers who focus on a single channel or traditional agencies that work in silos, Growth Engine is an integrated system where every component reinforces the others. You get a dedicated team strategist, SEO specialist, content writer, automation expert, and analyst all aligned on a single growth objective for your business.
